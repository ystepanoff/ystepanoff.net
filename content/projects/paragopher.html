ParaGopher

When I was a kid, I used to play this MS-DOS game called Paratrooper, and somehow when I stumbled upon Ebitengine 2D game engine, I remembered that game and decided to make something similar to satisfy my nostalgia. Ebitengine is exactly what it says on the tin: a dead simple game engine, so I had a working prototype within a couple of hours. Here I wrote down some notes about simple maths under the hood of ParaGopher.

Rotating and drawing the turret barrel

Finding the bullet's spawn position (tip of the barrel)

Computing bullet's velocity components

Updating bullet's position (basic kinematics)

Collision checks
